================================================================
            ARCANE ASCENDANT  -  Mage Action RPG
================================================================

HOW TO RUN
----------
Just double-click  ArcaneAscendant.exe
No installation, no DLLs, no internet required. 100% offline.
Works on Windows 10/11 (64-bit). Runs on integrated GPUs
(Intel HD / UHD, AMD Radeon Vega) - NO dedicated NVIDIA card needed.
Requires only OpenGL 3.3 (every PC from ~2012 onward supports it).

If Windows SmartScreen appears (because the exe is unsigned):
  click "More info" -> "Run anyway".


CONTROLS
--------
  W / A / S / D ....... Move (forward / left / back / right)
  Mouse ............... Rotate camera (look around)
  Mouse Wheel ......... Zoom camera in / out
  SPACE ............... Jump
  SHIFT ............... Sprint / run
  Q ................... Lock-on nearest enemy (press again to unlock)
  1 - 8 ............... Select a spell / power
  RIGHT MOUSE (hold) .. Aim the selected spell
  LEFT MOUSE .......... Cast / fire the selected spell
  T ................... Open / close the Ability Tree (spend skill points)
  I ................... Open / close the Inventory (use food/water/potions)
  R ................... Restart (after death or victory)
  ESC ................. Close menus


THE 8 SPELLS
------------
  1  CHAIN LINK   - Links nearby enemies; normal hits spread to all linked.
  2  FIRE BOLT    - Free, 2s cooldown. Your bread-and-butter fire bolt.
  3  FROST NOVA   - Freezes enemies in an area (timer shown above them).
  4  INFERNO      - Sets the ground ablaze; enemies burn over time.
  5  POISON CLOUD - Green poison. Stacks! More stacks = faster HP drain.
  6  GRASPING HANDS - Hands rise from the ground, grab & crush enemies.
  7  METEOR       - Huge fireball, hits up to 10 enemies, massive damage,
                    but costs a LOT of mana.
  8  BLEEDING DAGGER - Thrown blade causes bleeding. Stacks for more bleed.

  PASSIVES: invest skill points (press P in the Ability Tree) to raise
  Spell Power, Crit Chance, max HP and max Mana.


GOAL
----
Survive 5 waves of enemies that arrive phase by phase, then defeat the
ARCHLICH boss. The boss has 3 phases - it gets faster, summons minions,
and slams the ground as its health drops. Collect GOLD and XP from fallen
enemies, level up, upgrade your spells in the Ability Tree, and ascend!

Have fun, Mage.
